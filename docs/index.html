8 Steps to Play-and-Earn TFC

Step 1: Get MetaMask

Get MetaMask for your browser. This is the wallet extension we'll use to store tokens and sign transactions.

Step 2: Acquire $1 of POL

You need a little MATIC (Polygon's coin) to pay for a Civic uniqueness pass and transaction fees.

Recommended: Try switchere.com (they allow small $5 USD at a time, but require ID).

Step 3: QR Code Reader

On your phone, install a QR code reader.
Example: This QR Scanner for Android

Step 4: Civic Uniqueness Pass

Go to getpass.civic.com/status , click on CAPTCHA Verification and switch it to Uniqueness – Proof of Personhood.

Then connect your wallet (Mainnet → Polygon → MetaMask) and sign the transaction in the popup. Follow steps (choose phone) until you see a QR code.

Step 5: Scan & Face Verification

Use the phone's QR scanner to scan that code.
You’ll open a browser page to scan your face properly.

After that, go back to your desktop environment.

Step 6: DApp Onboarding

Visit hari-hari-dev.github.io/Grens/dapp.html
Press “Connect Wallet” and carefully pick a TFC username.
No spaces or special chars! Once typed, click Onboard.

Step 7: Metamask Gas Tweaks

If the Onboard transaction sticks or lags, you may need to adjust gas in MetaMask for a pending transaction.

Step 8: Verify Your Name

Once the transaction completes, use “Confirm Name” and “Check Registered Name” to be sure it’s recognized.

That’s it! You now earn Grens on the Tokenized TFC servers.
